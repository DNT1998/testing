How to register party ?

1. Go to website www.indgovvoting.com .

2. Goto party registration page .

3. Fill all details carefully .

4. Check all details which you fill are correct or not . If it will wrong then you are responsible for that .

5. Submitt the registration form .

6. Now you receive an OTP on your mobile number .

7. Fill the OTP & submitt and now you are successful register your party .

8. In 24-72 hour you get an email for your successful registration ( It take verification time that your all details are correct or not ) .

How to login ?

1. Go to website www.indgovvoting.com .

2. Goto voter login page .

3. Enter your name , DOB and aadhar number .

4. Now insert fingerpring and then login for vote .

5. Now you reach on detail page . Check their all details are your's or not .

6. If all details are your's then confiem page .

7. Now you reached on a pannel for vote .

8. Here you stay only 30seconds after that it is logout automaticaly and your id block for some time ( till the next voting time ) .
